## The GEO Content Framework for AI Overviews

### Question-First Content Architecture

Start every piece of content by identifying the exact questions your audience asks. Not broad topics. Specific questions.

Use these question formats that AI Overviews favour:

- "How to [specific task] for [specific audience]"
- "What is the difference between [A] and [B]"
- "Why does [specific problem] happen"
- "When should you [specific action]"

### Answer-Led Content Structure

AI Overviews scan for immediate, clear answers. Structure your content like this:

1. **Direct answer first** - Lead with the solution in 1-2 sentences
2. **Supporting evidence** - Add data, examples, or expert quotes
3. **Context and nuance** - Provide deeper explanation
4. **Related information** - Include relevant details

This structure increased citation rates by 156% across our tracked brands.

### Multi-Modal Content Optimisation

Google AI Overviews increasingly pull from video transcripts and image alt text. Optimise beyond text:

### Temporal Relevance Signals

Fresh content gets prioritised. Update existing high-performing content every 3-6 months with:

- New statistics or data points
- Recent case studies or examples

### Entity-Based Optimisation

AI systems understand entities (people, places, companies, concepts) better than keywords. Optimise for entities by:

- Clearly defining your brand entity in content
- Consistently mentioning related industry entities
- Building topical authority around core entities
- Creating comprehensive entity-focused content hubs
