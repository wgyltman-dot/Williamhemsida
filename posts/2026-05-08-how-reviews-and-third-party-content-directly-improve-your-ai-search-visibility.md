- Independent review sites (G2, Capterra, Trustpilot)
- Industry publications and case studies

- Customer success stories on third-party sites
- Analyst reports and comparisons

One client saw their Perplexity mentions jump 89% in six weeks just by optimizing their G2 profile and encouraging detailed customer reviews. The key? AI engines trust content they didn't pay for.

## The Third-Party Content Audit Framework

Before you optimize anything, map what's already out there. Use this systematic approach:

### Phase 1: Discovery
Search for your brand across these platforms:
- Review sites (G2, Capterra, TrustRadius)

- Comparison sites and "best of" lists

### Phase 2: Assessment

- **Authority**: Domain strength and site reputation

### Phase 3: Prioritization  
Focus on high-authority sites where you can influence the content. G2 profiles beat random blog mentions every time.

### G2 Profile Optimization Checklist

Your G2 profile is prime real estate for AI engines. Optimize it like this:

- [ ] Complete 100% of profile fields (AI engines favor comprehensive data)
- [ ] Include specific use cases in your description ("49% faster lead qualification", not "improves efficiency")  
- [ ] Upload screenshots showing actual results/dashboards
- [ ] Encourage customers to mention specific features and outcomes in reviews
- [ ] Respond to reviews with additional context and metrics
- [ ] Keep your competitor comparison grid updated monthly
- [ ] Add integration details and technical specifications

One B2B SaaS client increased their ChatGPT mentions by 156% after following this checklist. The key was getting customers to include specific metrics in their reviews.

## Content Structure That AI Engines Prefer

Third-party content needs specific formatting to maximize AI pickup:

### Use Comparison Tables
AI engines love structured comparisons. Encourage reviewers and partners to use formats like:

**Feature Comparison:**
- Ease of use: 9/10
- Integration options: 15+ native integrations
- Setup time: Under 30 minutes
- ROI timeline: 3-6 months

### Include Specific Metrics
Replace vague claims with concrete numbers:
- "Increased conversions by 34%" not "improved performance"
- "Reduced setup time from 3 hours to 20 minutes" not "faster implementation"
- "Handles 500K+ API calls daily" not "scales well"

### Provide Context and Use Cases
Help AI engines understand when to recommend your solution:
- Company size (50-500 employees)
- Industry focus (B2B SaaS, ecommerce)
- Specific problems solved
- Integration requirements
