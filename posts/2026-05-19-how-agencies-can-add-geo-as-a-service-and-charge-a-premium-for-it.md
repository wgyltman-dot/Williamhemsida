## Common Implementation Mistakes to Avoid

**Mistake 1: Treating GEO Like Traditional SEO**
AI engines prioritize different content signals. They prefer structured, factual content over keyword-optimized blog posts. Direct answers perform better than long-form content.

**Mistake 2: Focusing Only on Google AI**
ChatGPT and Perplexity have different content preferences and source priorities. A comprehensive GEO strategy addresses all major AI engines.

**Mistake 3: Neglecting Negative AI Mentions**
AI engines can surface negative information about brands. Monitoring and managing negative AI responses is crucial for brand protection.

**Mistake 4: Underpricing GEO Services**
GEO requires specialized expertise and tools. Agencies that price GEO like traditional SEO leave money on the table and devalue the service.
