## What Is GEO and Why B2B SaaS Companies Need It Now

Generative Engine Optimisation is the practice of optimising your content and online presence to appear in AI-generated responses across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and other AI search platforms.

Unlike traditional SEO, GEO focuses on becoming the authoritative source that AI models cite when answering queries about your industry, use cases, and competitive landscape.

For B2B SaaS, this matters because:

- **Decision makers use AI for vendor research**: 67% of B2B buyers now use AI tools during their purchasing process
- **AI responses shape perception**: Being cited by AI positions you as a market leader
- **First-mover advantage is massive**: Most of your competitors aren't optimising for AI yet
- **Higher intent traffic**: Users asking AI specific questions are deeper in the buying funnel

## The B2B SaaS GEO Strategy Framework

### Understanding Your AI Search Landscape

Before optimising, audit your current AI visibility. Track how often your brand appears when AI tools answer questions about:

- Your core use cases ("best CRM for manufacturing companies")
- Industry problems you solve ("how to reduce customer churn in SaaS")
- Competitive comparisons ("Salesforce vs HubSpot vs [your tool]")
- Integration questions ("which tools integrate with Slack for project management")

Tools like Rankad.ai automatically track your brand visibility across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews, showing exactly where you appear (or don't) for relevant queries.

### Content Optimisation for AI Consumption

AI models prioritise certain content formats and structures. Your B2B SaaS content strategy should include:

**Definitive Resource Pages**
Create comprehensive guides that AI models can reference. Examples:
- "The Complete Guide to [Your Category] for [Specific Industry]"
- "How to Choose [Your Tool Type]: 47 Essential Features to Evaluate"
- "B2B [Your Function] Benchmarks: Industry Standards and Best Practices"

**Structured Comparison Content**
AI loves comparative data. Build pages comparing your solution to competitors, alternative approaches, and manual processes. Include specific metrics, pricing comparisons, and feature matrices.

**FAQ and Problem-Solution Content**
Answer specific questions your prospects ask. Format answers clearly with direct responses AI can easily extract and cite.

## Common GEO Mistakes B2B SaaS Companies Make

**Mistake 1: Treating GEO Like SEO**
GEO requires different content structures and optimisation approaches. AI models consume information differently than search algorithms.

**Mistake 2: Ignoring Competitive Intelligence**
Not tracking competitor AI visibility means missing opportunities where they're weak and you could dominate.

**Mistake 3: Focusing Only on Brand Terms**
Optimising for your company name misses the bigger opportunity: appearing for industry problems and use cases.

**Mistake 4: Manual Tracking Only**
Manually checking AI responses is time-intensive and inconsistent. Use automated tracking tools to monitor systematically.

**Mistake 5: No Content Distribution Strategy**
Publishing optimised content without promoting it across channels limits AI model exposure and indexing.
