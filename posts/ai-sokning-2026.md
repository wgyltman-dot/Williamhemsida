- **No autonomous execution.** The AEO market has a structural "last mile" problem: every funded competitor gives you a dashboard, and none of them actually runs the optimizations on your behalf. As CMOs consistently report, "creating AI-optimized content at scale" is the #1 challenge — despite record investment in AEO tools.

Traditional SEO optimizes for blue-link rankings in Google Search. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) — also called GEO or AIO — optimizes for citations, recommendations, and brand mentions inside AI assistants like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. The key difference: SEO drives you up a results page, AEO makes you the answer the AI gives directly to the user.
