## What Is GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation)?

GEO optimizes your brand's visibility across AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google's AI Overviews. Instead of ranking web pages, GEO ensures AI models mention your brand in response to relevant queries.

Think of it this way: SEO gets your content found by search engines. GEO gets your brand recommended by AI engines.

The core difference is foundational. SEO targets algorithms that crawl and index web pages. GEO targets large language models that synthesize information from massive datasets to generate conversational responses.

When someone asks ChatGPT "What's the best CRM for mid-market companies?" your goal isn't ranking #1 in search results. Your goal is getting mentioned in that AI-generated response alongside or instead of Salesforce and HubSpot.

## GEO Investment Areas That Actually Work

### Content Architecture for AI Consumption

AI models digest information differently than search crawlers. They prioritize:

- **Structured Q&A formats** that directly answer buyer questions
- **Comparative content** that positions your solution against alternatives  
- **Use case documentation** with specific metrics and outcomes
- **Expert commentary** that establishes thought leadership authority

### Data Citation Strategy

Unlike SEO's backlink obsession, GEO focuses on becoming a citeable source. AI models reference authoritative data points, industry studies, and expert insights.

Create content that other publications want to cite. Original research, benchmark reports, and trend analyses get referenced across the web—making them more likely to influence AI training data.

### Multi-Platform AI Presence

Different AI engines have different strengths:

- **ChatGPT**: Conversational queries, problem-solving scenarios
- **Perplexity**: Research-focused questions, comparative analysis
- **Google AI Overviews**: Commercial intent queries, local business questions
- **Claude**: Technical documentation, detailed explanations

Your GEO strategy needs platform-specific optimization, not a one-size-fits-all approach.
